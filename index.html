Location

The exclusive complex of Sobha Seahaven will be located in Dubai Harbour. Residents will be able to get to other areas of the emirate, as well as to key attractions and landmarks of the emirate, in just a several-minute drive thanks to its direct access to the Sheikh Zayed Road. For instance, Downtown Dubai with the iconic Burj Khalifa, Dubai Mall and Business Bay, which is the business centre of the emirate, are all reachable within a 20-minute drive.

Among the nearest most well-known dining venues are Dinner In The Sky, Maya Mexican Kitchen + Bar, Bellavista, Blue Jade, Deniz Restaurant and Cafe, Lamoroso Restaurant & Cafe, and many more. In terms of shopping, Dubai Marina Mall which houses 140 retail stores, 21 dining options and Reel Cinemas is one of the closest options for residents to enjoy. You might also be interested in Nakheel Mall on Palm Jumeirah, where apart from retail stores, cafes and restaurants, you can also find entertainment activities, such as Trampo Extreme and FabyLand.

Residents of the premium development might also be interested in Aquaventure Waterpark, located at the top of Palm Jumeirah. Here you will find over 105 rip-roaring slides, including attractions such as Zoomerango, Shockwave, Odyssey of Terror, and many more. The Lost Chambers Aquarium offers 21 exhibits to explore, and is home to 65,000 marine creatures. Sports enthusiasts will also appreciate Skydive Dubai, offering unforgettable experiences such as tandem skydive, indoor skydiving, gyrocopter flights and more.

Sobha Seahaven is planned to be completed in Q4 2026. This elite complex is an ideal option for permanent living on the coast of the Arabian Gulf, as well as a good choice for holiday homes. You will always be able to resell your property at a higher price after its handover.
